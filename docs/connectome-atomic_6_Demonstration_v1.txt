CONNECTOME-ATOMIC DEMONSTRATION  (v1)

This is the Demonstration volume for the connectome-atomic repository. It is the
owner's addition to the five SPARC phases (Specification, Pseudocode,
Architecture, Refinement, Completion), not a sixth SPARC phase. It is a PLAN of
intent for demonstrating the repository: what to run, what each run shows, which
capabilities it exhibits, and what a viewer should conclude. It is a plan, not a
finished demonstration.

The repository is FROZEN; this file DESCRIBES how one would demonstrate the
built, immutable code and changes nothing. Acronyms are spelled out on first use.
Author: the PrologAI Community.


THE ONE THING THIS DEMONSTRATION MUST LAND

A viewer should leave convinced of a single claim: the SAME anatomy, carved a
DIFFERENT way, behaves and grounds IDENTICALLY, and the price of the maximal-
granularity carving is visible, measured, and honestly reported. The demo is not
a product pitch; it is the presentation of a controlled measurement.


PREREQUISITES TO SET UP BEFORE DEMONSTRATING

• SWI-Prolog version nine installed.
• A local PrologAI checkout reachable through the environment variable
  PROLOGAI_HOME (default /home/ccaitwo/PrologAI), reused read-only and unmodified.
• A terminal at the repository root. No build step is needed; the gates assemble
  the library path themselves.


THE DEMONSTRATION SEQUENCE (WHAT TO RUN, AND WHAT EACH SHOWS)

Run the five gates in order. Each exits zero on success, so the whole sequence is
legible even to a viewer who does not read Prolog.

Step one - the reentrant loop and its glass-box trace.
  Run: bin/run_slice.sh 8 5 0.4   (eight laps, a cortisol event on lap five,
  learning rate zero point four).
  What it shows: a live, narrated trace, one line per hop. Point the viewer at
  three things. First, the phrase "via lattice" on EVERY hop - the beat always
  arrives through the shared Lattice, never from a named sender, including the
  reentrant thalamus-to-cortex return. Second, the token rising by exactly one
  each hop, and the closing verdict proving the loop ran one through three times
  N plus one, in the exact region sequence, with the cortex re-entering N times.
  Third, the dopamine reward-prediction-error falling from one toward zero as the
  cortex's prediction rises to meet the reward, and - after the one-line CORTISOL
  EVENT banner on lap five - the weight barely moving despite ongoing reward, the
  glucocorticoid tone suppressing plasticity across a ten-stratum skip.

Step two - the strict layer rule.
  Run: bin/check_layers.sh
  What it shows: PrologAI's UNMODIFIED layer construct, run over the arm's eleven
  packs, reporting zero upward edges across six levels. The takeaway: the tallest,
  most-packed of the three arms still obeys the layer rule cleanly.

Step three - the zero-coupling proof.
  Run: bin/check_no_coupling.sh
  What it shows: the three region packs share ONLY the Lattice - zero
  actor-to-actor references in code. The takeaway: the reentrant loop closes
  without any region ever naming, importing, or addressing another; the upward
  return lives only at runtime, as a numbered phase cue.

Step four - the Causalontology grounding.
  Run: bin/validate_structure.sh
  What it shows: all twenty-eight structure records, minted across nine packs,
  validating against the Causalontology 3.0.0 schemas and semantics; the cortisol
  causal_relation_object classifying as skipping with no skip-gap; and the
  Ed25519-signed provenance assertion verifying. The takeaway: distributing the
  minting cost the data nothing - a strong result for the content-addressed
  identity model.

Step five - the in-pack tests.
  Run: bin/run_tests.sh
  What it shows: every one of the eleven packs' in-pack test suites (the PrologAI
  Unit framework, PLUnit) passing. The takeaway: no pack rots silently; each
  ships its own test from its first commit.

Optional step six - the mini regression.
  Run the ten percent spot-check of the two solved benchmarks. What it shows,
  stated honestly: the Abstraction and Reasoning Corpus for Artificial General
  Intelligence, first generation (ARC-AGI-1), scoring forty of forty sampled
  tasks, and the second generation (ARC-AGI-2), scoring twelve of twelve sampled
  tasks. Say plainly that this is a ten percent sample and that the full
  regression is deferred; do NOT present it as a full four hundred of four hundred
  or one hundred twenty of one hundred twenty score.


THE TWO IDENTITY PROOFS TO SHOW ALONGSIDE

The gates prove the arm WORKS. Two comparisons prove the arm changed nothing but
the pack boundaries, and they are the heart of a control-arm demonstration:

• Behavioural identity. Show that the narrated trace from step one is
  byte-identical to the Wave 2 slice's trace, by a direct comparison with the Git
  "diff" command. Same hops, tokens, weights, dopamine decay, cortisol event.

• Data identity. Show that the twenty-eight records written to structure/ have
  content-addressed identifiers byte-identical to the slice's. Distributing the
  minting across nine packs produced the same identifiers.


THE CAPABILITIES A VIEWER SHOULD SEE EXHIBITED

• Stigmergic coordination - three background threads closing a reentrant loop
  through one shared Lattice, with zero direct references and no busy-poll.
• Glass-box legibility - every hop, every number, and the layer skip narrated in
  plain language as it happens.
• Causalontology 3.0.0 grounding - twenty-eight validated records across ten
  kinds, content-addressed and signed, minted by distributed construct packs.
• Disciplined decomposition - a six-level layer stack that obeys the strict rule
  under the heaviest pack count of the three arms.
• Honest measurement - the LEDGER's seven open findings and the COMPARISON's
  eight-axis rubric, presented as the actual product.


WHAT A VIEWER SHOULD CONCLUDE

• That one pack per named construct buys the best possible change locality:
  retune any single construct (for example, dopamine's reward schedule) and touch
  exactly one pack.
• That the price is real and was measured, not hidden: coupling grows faster than
  pack count, concentrated at the interfaces; grounding fit is low because
  construct boundaries cut across the Causalontology kinds; and per-pack ceremony
  dominates at scale.
• That the platform held (behaviour, data, the layer rule, the closure discipline
  all passed) while surfacing four genuinely new packaging gaps and re-confirming
  three known layer-construct limits.
• That this arm is FROZEN EVIDENCE - a preserved measurement whose value is that
  it recorded exactly what maximal granularity revealed, and whose verdict within
  the three-arm experiment is completed only when the loops arm and the strata arm
  are read beside it.

The demonstration succeeds if a viewer can say, in one breath: the code ran, the
behaviour and the data were proven identical to the slice, and the cost of
carving one pack per construct was shown plainly rather than sold.
